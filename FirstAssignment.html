What is UI?

It allows users to interact with a device, webpage, or app

A user interface (UI) developer designs and builds the visual and interactive elements of digital products. This includes designing and building interfaces, such as buttons, menus, and forms, that users interact with. UI developers use design principles combined with technical expertise and an understanding of human psychology to develop an easy and enjoyable experience between a human user and a digital application.

    UI development employers typically require candidates to have at least a bachelor's degree, with a major in:

  1. Computer Science
  2. Web Development
  3. Human-Computer Interaction
  4. Mobile Computing
  5. Graphic Design
  6. Software Engineering
  7. A related field of study
